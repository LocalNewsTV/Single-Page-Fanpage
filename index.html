2B: Heroine

2B is a battle android developed by YorHa. She is deployed to combat the machine lifeforms that have invaded Earth, on behalf of the surviving humans.

9S: 2B's Partner

9S is a scanner android developed by YorHa. His model is distinct from 2B's and lacks the combat prowess, so he supports 2B's mission using his intel.

A2: Exiled Android

A2 is a mysterious antagonist that confronts 2B and 9S during their mission against the machine lifeforms. Despite being exiled from YorHa, A2 retains her hostility against the machines.

The Story of Nier So Far.

Set during a proxy war between alien-created machines and human-crafted androids, the story follows the trials of androids 9S, 2B and A2. The story requires multiple playthroughs, each unlocking additional story elements. Gameplay combines role-playing elements with action-based hack and slash combat, and features switches between video game genres similar to that of Nier with elements ranging from shoot 'em up to text adventure.
